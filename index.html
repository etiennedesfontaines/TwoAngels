Family Suite

Breathtaking beauty is on your doorstep…
Family Suite is your unforgettable holiday for six.
Savor magnificent sunsets right from your dining room table. Or sink into the comfort of your private daybed and indulge in a book, while listening to your children laughing and playing safely on the beach below.

The Angel's Lair

Serenity surrounds you…
The Angel's Lair is your family break for four.
Treat your loved ones to a paradise whose privacy is uniquely created by nature. Soothe your soul to the sound of the sea rolling in. And share a restful afternoon gazing over the lagoon from the comfort of your private daybed.

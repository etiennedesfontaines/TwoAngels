Activities

Looking for adventure? Relaxation? Culture?
Whatever your interests may be, Knysna has you covered.

Whether you're a nature lover or a foodie, a thrill-seeker or someone who prefers a more laid-back experience, there's something for everyone in this picturesque town. Take a hike through the lush forests of the Garden Route National Park, explore the breathtaking coastline on a guided kayak tour, head through the heads for some Whale watching, try your hand zip-lining through the treetops, or simply take a stroll around town and enjoy the many local restaurants.

So why wait? Come and create unforgettable memories in Knysna.

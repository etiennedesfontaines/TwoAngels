Privacy Policy

At TwoAngels Guest House, we take the privacy of our customers seriously. This privacy policy outlines the ways in which we collect, use, and protect personal information that is provided to us through our website, twoangels.co.za.

What information we collect:

When you use our contact form to make an inquiry, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and any message you include in the contact form.

How we use your information:

We use the information you provide to respond to your enquiry and to provide you with the information you requested. We may also use your information to send you promotional or marketing materials related to TwoAngels Guest House, unless you opt-out of such communications.

We do not share your personal information with third parties unless required by law, or to comply with a court order or other legal process.

Consent to use your information:

When you submit your enquiry using our contact form, you are required to check a box that indicates that you have read and agree to this privacy policy. By checking this box, you are giving us your consent to use your personal information for the purposes outlined in this policy.

Data security:

We take appropriate measures to ensure the security of your personal information. We use industry-standard encryption and security technologies to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or modification.

Data retention:

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law.

Your rights:

You have the right to request access to the personal information we have collected about you, to request that we update or correct any inaccuracies, or to request that we delete your information. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided on our website.

Changes to our privacy policy:

We reserve the right to modify or update this privacy policy at any time. Any changes we make will be posted on this page.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at bassecour.rd@mweb.co.za.

Agreement:

By using our website and submitting your personal information through our contact form, you agree to the terms outlined in this privacy policy.
